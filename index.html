1

Your Profit Reality Check

We'll cut through the noise and show you the real profit picture. Not what your software says, not what your accountant reported last year, but what's actually happening right now. You'll identify your biggest profit leaks and the hidden costs eating into your margins.

Cash Flow Mastery for the New Tax Year

Using DentPulse's MAP Method (Manage, Analyse, Project), you'll map your cash flow for the coming year. You'll learn to handle NHS payment lags, private plan timing, and seasonal dips so you always know what's coming in and going out.
